Christmas in Tanzania

Christmas Day is an annual holiday in Tanzania, and many people celebrate Christmas by travelling back from the cities to the villages
to spend time with their parents and grandparents. Road travel peaks from around two weeks before December 25th.
About three days before Christmas, people start decorating their homes.

Discover ways to enjoy your Christmas holidays in Tanzania from the snowy peak mountain in Kilimanjaro to the beautiful Zanzibar Island.
